    class Contact

    A {Contact} is an ordered pair of {Actor Actors}, and therefore two {SocialStream::Models::Subject Subjects}.

    {Contact Contacts} are created at convenience (in the case of {Actor#suggestions suggestions}, for instance), and they do not mean that there is a real link between those two {SocialStream::Models::Subject Subjects}. Link existance is stored as a {Tie}. When Alice adds Bob as contact, a new {Tie} is created with the {Contact} from Alice to Bob and the {Relation} that Alice chose.

    Inverse Contacts¶ ↑

    Alice has a {Contact} to Bob. The inverse is the {Contact} from {Bob} to {Alice}. Inverse contacts are used to check if contacts are replied, for instance, if Bob added Alice as contact after she did so.

    Again, the Contact from Bob to Alice must have positive {Tie ties} to be active.
